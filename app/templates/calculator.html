About this engine

The original payroll math lived in the PowerBuilder client, not the database. These formulas were reverse-engineered from 352k rows of closed payroll in paytranh and verified to the centavo:

  • SSS — table lookup (ssstable), employee share by monthly gross.
  • PhilHealth — 2.5% of monthly salary (5% total), floor ₱10k / ceiling ₱100k.
  • Pag-IBIG — 2% of salary, employee share capped at ₱200.
  • Withholding tax — BIR TRAIN bracket table (taxtable) on per-period taxable.

See Verify Engine vs History for the match rate against actual stored payroll.
